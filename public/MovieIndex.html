The Best Movies of All Time by Cathy

My top three movies

Harry Potter Series

Harry Potter is an amazing movie series filled with magic and fantasy where good prevails over evil.

Big

Big is just so creative and ahead of its time. It was the first movie to trap an adult in a child's body and Tom Hanks set the bar high.

Charlie's Angels

Smart, beautiful, independent women who can be strong and compassionate at the same time.
